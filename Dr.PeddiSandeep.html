Dr. Peddi Sandeep

Dr Peddi Sandeep has a vast clinical experience of more than 12 years in practise of Nephrology.

He is experienced with profound skills to deal with kidney diseases like Chronic Kidney diseases, Hypertension, kidney issues associated with Diabetes, Hemodialysis, Peritoneal Dialysis.

He has done more than 800 renal biopsies in last 12 years. He manages patients with various poisoning by a variety of Extracorporeal treatment (Hemoperfussion/Hemodiafiltration).

He heads the Department of Nephrology at Chalmeda Ananda Rao Institute of Medical Sciences. He has trained more than 30 MD General Medicine doctors (PG) during his tenure.

He has more than 30 publications to his credit in various National and International journals.
