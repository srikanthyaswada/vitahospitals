Emergency Services Include:

  • Emergency Room (ER) or Emergency Department (ED): Well equipped and planned Emergency Room under management of 24 hour available emergency physician, well trained staff, well equipped infrastructure.
  • Provides immediate medical care for acute illnesses and injuries like trauma, cardiac care, brain stroke care, pediatric emergencies, emergency labour and delivery services.
  • Beside portable power backup ventilator services for emergency intubation of necessary.
  • Portable dialysis machinery to provide for emergency hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis for various renal diseases and poisoning cases.
  • Minor surgical OT fully functional and linked to the ER to carry out trauma related, Daycare and basic surgical procedures.

Radiology

  • Our radiology services include CT scan, Xray, ultrasound, 2D echo
  • Available 24*7, with full time In-house radiologist and well trained staff.
  • Beside portable Xray, ultrasound and 2D echo facilities.

Scope Of Surgical Gastroenterology Services:

  • Complicated Appendectomy: Re-do surgeries, abscess, appendicular mass
  • Complicated Cholecystectomy: Perforated gall bladder, chronic cholecystitis, tumors of gallbladder.
  • Bileduct stones, stenting, Injuries and repair.
  • Hernia repair: recurrent hernias, huge incisional hernias, laparoscopic hernia repairs, diaphragmatic and hiatal hernia.
  • Colorectal procedures: laparoscopic Colectomy, diagnostic & theraupetic Colonoscopy, colon biopsies, colorectal cancer.
  • Esophageal surgery: for cancer, reflux disease (GERD), or achalasia (swallowing disorder).
  • Liver surgeries: liver cancer, liver cysts, and liver transplantation.
  • Pancreatic surgery: for cancer, pancreatitis, or pancreatic cysts/stones.
  • Bariatric surgery: weight loss, in severe obesity and related health issues.
  • Endoscopic procedures: polyp removal, dilation of strictures, and placement of stents.

Surgical Oncology

  • Diagnostic Surgery: biopsies accurate cancer diagnosis.
  • Curative Surgery: complete removal of cancer tumors.
  • Head and neck, breast, thyroid, ovarian, intestinal and soft tissue cancers
  • Debulking Surgery: to reduce the size of the tumor and improve the effectiveness of chemotherapy or radiation therapy.
  • Lymph Node Dissection: to check for and control cancer spread.
  • Reconstructive Surgery: to restore the appearance and function of affected areas.
  • Palliative Surgery: improving quality of life in advanced cancer.
  • Peritoneal Malignancy: treatment of cancers of the abdominal cavity.

Rooms- VIP rooms, private rooms and sharing rooms.
